1. Rules and Regulations

1. Lab Space Booking:

  1. All bookings must be made in advance using this form. Walk-in requests will be considered if the lab is available, but priority will be given to those who have made prior reservations.

2. Booking Duration:

  1. The maximum booking duration is 2 hours for regular use.
  2. Extensions may be requested but will be subject to availability and approval from the lab supervisor.

3. Booking Cancellation:

  1. If you need to cancel your booking, please inform the laboratory staff at least 1 day in advance. Failure to do so may impact your ability to book future sessions.

4. Lab Equipment Usage:

  1. No equipment rentals are allowed for external use. All equipment should only be used within the lab during the booked session.
  2. All lab equipment (e.g., computers, networking devices, routers) must be handled with care. Follow all safety and usage guidelines provided by the lab staff.
  3. Any damage or malfunction of equipment must be immediately reported to the lab supervisor.

5. Behavioral Expectations:

  1. Users must maintain a quiet, respectful environment in the lab to ensure productivity for all.
  2. No food or drinks are allowed in the lab.
  3. Users are expected to clean up after themselves and leave the lab in good condition for the next user.
